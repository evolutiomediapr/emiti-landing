Emiti SMS Payment Reminders

Consent & Opt-In Process

This page documents, for carrier and A2P 10DLC (TCR) review, how end users consent to receive SMS from this messaging program. Last updated: July 7, 2026.

1. Program overview

Emiti is an invoicing platform (SaaS) used by contractors and small businesses in Puerto Rico. Through Emiti, a business can send transactional payment reminders by SMS to its own clients about overdue invoices. Messages are sent via our messaging provider, Twilio.

This is not a marketing program. No promotional, advertising, or sales content is ever sent. Each message concerns a specific overdue invoice the recipient already received from the business.

2. Who sends and who receives (B2B2C model)

This is a standard on-behalf (B2B2C) model for a SaaS platform serving small businesses:

  • Sender: a small business or contractor that uses Emiti (the platform's user).
  • Recipient: that business's own client — a person who already received an invoice from the business for services rendered.
  • Emiti's role: the software platform that sends the SMS on the business's behalf, only for recipients whose consent the business has recorded.

3. How consent is obtained

Consent is collected by the business directly from its own client, and confirmed inside the Emiti app before any reminder can be sent:

  1. The business obtains permission from its client directly (verbally or in writing at the time of service) to send SMS reminders about the client's invoices.
  2. When saving the client's phone number, the business may check an optional, unchecked-by-default checkbox in the app expressly confirming that the client agreed to receive these SMS. The checkbox never blocks saving — the business can save the document and continue without checking it — but SMS reminders are only enabled for clients whose consent was recorded this way.
  3. Each consent is recorded immutably with a timestamp, the account that granted it, and the method (stored in an append-only sms_consents ledger). Emiti only generates automated reminders for phone numbers that have this recorded consent.

Because consent is captured in-app (behind the business's login), the exact wording, the optional checkbox, and screenshots of the consent screen are published below so a reviewer can verify the full opt-in experience.

4. Exact opt-in wording (as shown in the app)

The consent checkbox presented to the business reads exactly as follows. The app is in Spanish (the market is Puerto Rico); an English translation is provided for review:

Spanish (as shown in app)

“Este cliente autorizó recibir recordatorios de pago por SMS de Emiti sobre sus facturas. Al marcar, confirma que su cliente aceptó recibir estos mensajes enviados por Emiti.”

English (translation)

“This client authorized receiving SMS payment reminders from Emiti about their invoices. By checking, you confirm your client agreed to receive these messages sent by Emiti.”

6. Message program details

Program name
Emiti SMS Payment Reminders
Purpose
Transactional reminders about overdue invoices. Not marketing.
Message frequency
Up to 1 message per overdue invoice; frequency varies with each business's overdue invoices.
Rates
Message and data rates may apply.
Opt-out & help
Recipients can reply STOP at any time to cancel (processed immediately), START to resume, or HELP for assistance.
No third-party sharing
Phone numbers are used only to send these transactional reminders. They are never shared or sold to third parties for marketing or any other purpose.
